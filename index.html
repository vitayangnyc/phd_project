Decoding Consumer Psychology in Immersive Marketing

An AI-Generative Framework Using Performing Arts Approaches to Orchestrate and Predict Brand Experience Outcomes.

This project aims to elaborate consumer behavior psychology by focusing on four progressive core areas. Each systematically builds on the previous research and provides the empirical foundation for the subsequent investigations, culminating in an AI-powered framework for brand experience design.

Four Core Research Areas

1. Creating the Stage

Transform theatrical elements-performers, dialogue, lighting, and props-into experiential marketing essentials, to orchestrate brand-consumer interactive scenarios and form actionable decision cues.

2. Refocusing the Narrative

Applying psychological Character Development techniques from Stanislavsky's Method Acting constructs complex customer profiles by delving into their sociocultural contexts, motivations, and emotional states to better target consumer identity and enable judgment prediction.

3. Scripting Journeys

By adapting the Standardized Patient (SP) methodology from medical education, this research will structure controlled interaction progressions. The systematic rehearsals will generate comprehensive datasets for precise touchpoint optimization.

4. AI-Generative Characterizing

This part of the research integrates the frameworks and data to train an Al model that automatically generates brand archetypes and dynamic brand encounter scripts to manage experiences, encourage authentic engagement, and drive value creation.
